Our Vision
Curiosity killed the cat, but satisfaction bought it back again! Curiosity is the only motive that pushs people towards their limits. It was the key that forced Sir/ Albert Einstein to discover what we call in physics the most original human work, and what encouraged Madam Curie to change the way that scientific community thinks of female. With all enthusiastic, we aspire to take the ensign of flaming the curiousity between high school students and below toward physics. We quarrel to make physics accessible for everyone with a simple yet informative review articles about different topics in physics. We aspire to change.

About Us
Gharbiya STEM Physics Journal
      The first physics review articles website held up by Gharbiya STEM high school students from Egypt. It will provide pi-weekly review articles discussing specific topics from different physics fields. The website will give the physics enthusiastics the opportunity to explore different advanced concepts in a simple, and concluding way that will intellectually entertain them.
